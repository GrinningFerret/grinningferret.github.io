All my builds include my modded kernel "StormySea".

Here a small feature list:

  • Compiled with the lastest UBERTC 4.9.4 optimized for Cortex-A7 CPUs

  • Additional CPU governors: Pegasusq, Intelliactive and Zzmoove

  • Additional I/O schedulers: SIO, FIOPS, TRIPNDROID and ZEN

  • IntelliPlug by faux123

  • Alucard hotplug

  • Support for FauxSoundControl

  • Additional TCP algorithms: bic, westwood and htcp

  • Simple GPU Algorithm by faux123

  • USB Fast Charge

  • Underclocked CPU to 190 MHz
